Purandar Fort


Overview

Purandar Fort, located in the Pune district of Maharashtra, is known for its rich history and strategic location. The fort is notable for its role in the Maratha Empire and its association with Chhatrapati Shivaji Maharaj.

History

Purandar Fort was built in the 13th century and became an important military outpost for the Marathas. It is famously known as the site where Shivaji Maharaj was formally recognized as the leader of the Maratha Empire.
