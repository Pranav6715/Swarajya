Lohagad Fort


Overview

Lohagad Fort, located near Lonavala in Maharashtra, is renowned for its historic significance and magnificent views of the Western Ghats. This fort is a favorite among trekkers and history enthusiasts alike.

History

Lohagad Fort was built during the 18th century and served as an important military stronghold for the Maratha Empire. It is famous for being captured by Chhatrapati Shivaji Maharaj and has been a site of many historical battles.

Difficulty Level and Useful Tips

The trek to Lohagad Fort is considered easy to moderate, taking about 1-2 hours to reach the summit. The path is well-marked, but wearing comfortable trekking shoes, carrying sufficient water, and visiting during the monsoon or winter months is recommended for the best experience.

What to See on the Fort?

  • Magnificent views of the surrounding valleys and hills
  • Ancient fortifications and the iconic Lohagad shoulder
  • The impressive Vinchu Kata (Scorpion's tail)
  • Historical temples within the fort premises
  • Stunning sunrise and sunset views
