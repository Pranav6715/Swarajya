Pratapgad Fort


Overview

Pratapgad Fort, located in the Satara district of Maharashtra, is known for its historical significance and stunning architecture. The fort is famous for the battle between Chhatrapati Shivaji Maharaj and Afzal Khan.

History

Built in 1656 by Shivaji Maharaj, Pratapgad Fort played a crucial role in the Maratha Empire's defense strategy. It is also notable for the strategic victory of Shivaji over Afzal Khan in 1659.

Difficulty Level and Useful Tips

The trek to Pratapgad Fort is moderate, usually taking about 1 to 2 hours. It is advisable to wear comfortable shoes, carry sufficient water, and start early in the day to avoid the afternoon heat.
