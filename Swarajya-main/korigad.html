Korigad Fort


Overview

Korigad Fort, also known as Koraigad, is situated in the Lonavala region of Maharashtra. This hill fort is renowned for its historical significance and stunning views of the surrounding landscape, including the nearby Pawna Lake. It's a popular destination for trekkers and adventure enthusiasts.

History

Korigad Fort was built during the reign of the Maratha Empire and is believed to have been constructed in the 18th century. The fort played a crucial role in the defense of the region and was captured by Chhatrapati Shivaji Maharaj. Today, it stands as a testament to the region's rich history.
