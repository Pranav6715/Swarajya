Tung Fort


Overview

Tung Fort, also known as Tungadi, is situated in the Lonavala region of Maharashtra. This fort is famous for its unique shape and panoramic views of the surrounding landscape, making it a popular trekking destination for adventure seekers.

History

Tung Fort is believed to have been constructed in the late 16th century during the reign of the Maratha Empire. The fort played a significant role in the defense of the region and is known for its strategic location.

Difficulty Level and Useful Tips

The trek to Tung Fort is considered moderate and typically takes about 1-2 hours to complete. The path may have steep sections, so it's essential to wear proper trekking shoes, carry enough water, and consider visiting during the monsoon or winter seasons for the best experience.

What to See on the Fort?

  • Stunning views of the surrounding valleys
  • Ancient fortifications and ruins
  • Beautiful sunrise and sunset points
  • Unique rock formations
  • Panoramic views of the nearby Tung and Bhira dams
